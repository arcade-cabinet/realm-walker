# World Names - The Geography of Aetherion

**Version**: 1.0  
**Status**: WORLD BIBLE  
**Created**: October 6, 2025  
**Purpose**: Name everything in the world

---

## The World

**Name**: **Aetherion**

**Meaning**: "Realm of eternal twilight" (Aether = upper air, -ion = place)

**Before the Sundering**: Vibrant, alive, four seasons, natural sun

**After the Sundering**: Eternal twilight, magical survival, desperate factions

---

## The Continents

### Lumina (Northern Continent)
**The Light Lands**

**Regions**:
- **The Radiant Highlands** - Dawnshield territory (central mountains)
- **The Iron Peaks** - Ironbound territory (volcanic mountains)
- **The Verdant Reach** - Verdant Spirits territory (ancient forests)
- **The Twilight Veil** - Veilwalkers territory (misty valleys)

**Climate**: Colder north, warmer where factions provide heat

### Umbra (Southern Continent)
**The Shadow Lands**

**Regions**:
- **The Scorched Wastes** - Eclipsed Flame territory (volcanic badlands)
- **The Crimson Marches** - Crimson Pact territory (Gothic lowlands)
- **The Resonance Depths** - Ashen Choir territory (underground caverns)
- **The Surgical Districts** - Ossuary Praxis territory (Victorian cities)

**Climate**: Warmer south (volcanic), colder where magic fails

### The Shattered Isles (Western Ocean)
**The Chaos Seas**

**Regions**:
- **The Hidden Coves** - Freebooter Corsairs (secret jungle islands)
- **The Otter Archipelago** - River otter trading network
- **Storm's End** - Badger construction hub
- **Scholar's Rest** - Fox archive island

**Climate**: Tropical (volcanic islands), naturally warm

### The Void Wastes (Eastern Expanse)
**The Broken Lands**

**Regions**:
- **The Null Zones** - Children of Hollow territory (reality tears)
- **The Profane Abyss** - Veilbound Synod territory (underground)
- **The Frozen Wastes** - No faction (instant death, pre-Sundering ruins)

**Climate**: Deadly cold except where void/eldritch provides warmth

## The Floating Realms

### The Crystal Spires (Sky)
**Above all continents**

- **The Luminous Spires** - Radiant Collegium territory
- **The Foundry Platforms** - Sanctum of Invention territory

**Tethered to**: Mountain peaks across Lumina and Umbra

---

## The Oceans

### The River of Stars (Central)
**Connects all continents**

- Otter trading routes
- Major waterway
- Frozen in places, flowing where magic warms it

### The Crimson Sea (South)
**Between Umbra and Shattered Isles**

- Blood-warm (Crimson Pact magic)
- Pirate waters
- Dangerous, profitable

### The Mist Ocean (West)
**Surrounds Shattered Isles**

- Permanent fog
- Hides the Corsair islands
- Only accessible by portal

### The Void Gulf (East)
**Reality-torn waters**

- Not really water anymore
- Void-touched
- Instant death for non-Hollow

## The Creatures

### Natural Wildlife (Not Monsters)

**Forest Creatures**:
- **Silverback Elk** (extinct, Thalindra's 1st scar)
- **Twilight Deer** - Adapted to darkness, bioluminescent antlers
- **Shadow Foxes** - Hunt at night, intelligent
- **Glowmoths** - Provide light, endangered (48th scar quest)

**Mountain Creatures**:
- **Stone Rams** - Hardy, survive cold
- **Cave Bats** - Echolocation, harmless
- **Frost Wolves** - Pack hunters, dangerous

**River Creatures**:
- **Giant Otters** - Ancestors of otter traders
- **Blind Fish** - Underground lakes
- **River Serpents** - Rare, dangerous

### Adapted Creatures (Post-Sundering)

**Corrupted by Magic**:
- **Eldritch Wolves** - Touched by Veilbound rifts, tentacles
- **Blood Bats** - Feed on Crimson magic, vampiric
- **Flame Lizards** - Thrive in Scorched Wastes, fire-breathing
- **Void Wraiths** - Half-real, Hollow-touched

**Created by Factions**:
- **Holy Constructs** - Sanctum creations, sentient
- **Flesh Golems** - Ossuary experiments, tragic
- **Sound Elementals** - Ashen manifestations, living music
- **Nature Spirits** - Verdant summons, forest guardians

### True Monsters (Hostile)

**Sundering-Born**:
- **Reality Tears** - Void manifestations, kill on sight
- **Shard Wraiths** - Drawn to shard energy, dangerous
- **Twilight Horrors** - Born from eternal darkness
- **Corruption Spawn** - Eldritch/blood/void hybrids

**Ancient Guardians** (Boss-tier):
- **The Sundering Witness** - Was there when sun broke, insane
- **The First Void** - Original reality tear, sentient
- **The Eternal Flame** - Living fire elemental, ancient
- **The World Tree's Rage** - Thalindra's anger manifest

## The Landmarks

### Pre-Sundering Ruins

**The Capital** (Frozen Wastes):
- Name: **Solaria** (was world capital)
- Population: 2 million (all dead, frozen instantly)
- Ruins: Perfectly preserved in ice
- Treasure: Pre-Sundering artifacts
- Danger: Extreme cold, no magic reaches here

**The Great Observatory** (Radiant territory):
- Where Luminara calculated the Sundering
- Still functional
- Houses the Restoration Equations

**The World Tree** (Verdant territory):
- Name: **Yggdrasil's Daughter**
- Age: 10,000+ years
- Thalindra merged with it
- Roots reach planetary core

### Post-Sundering Monuments

**The Sundering Ground** (Central, contested):
- Where it happened
- Reality is scarred
- All factions want it
- WAR finale location

**The 40-Day Memorial** (Ironbound/Crimson border):
- Where Thrain held the line
- 253 dwarven graves
- Crimson Pact memorial opposite (10,000 vampires fell)
- Neutral ground, both sides honor it

**The First Rift** (Veilbound territory):
- Where Seraph opened first reality tear
- Still active, still dangerous
- Pilgrimage site for cultists

## The Story Arc

### Emotional Beats (Major)

**Act 1: Innocence Lost**
- Tutorial village (peace)
- The Raid (betrayal/loyalty choice)
- First faction (hope or power)

**Act 2: The Journey**
- Meet factions (understand perspectives)
- Impossible choices (no right answers)
- Build warband (earn loyalty)
- Collect shards (prove worthy)

**Act 3: The Reckoning**
- All factions converge
- The Sunderer reveals itself
- Final choice (restore, consume, destroy, compromise)
- Consequences (see what you've wrought)

### Character Arc (Player)

**Beginning**: Naive wanderer, sees good vs evil  
**Middle**: Understands complexity, questions everything  
**End**: Makes informed choice, accepts consequences

**Growth**: From innocent to wise, from simple to complex
