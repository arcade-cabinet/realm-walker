# World Lore

**Version**: 1.0 **FROZEN**

## Setting Overview

Realm Walker Story takes place in a **Gothic fantasy world** where supernatural forces intersect with Victorian-era society. The world is characterized by its eerie atmosphere, blood-red color schemes, and the constant tension between the mundane and the mystical.

## Visual Aesthetic

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Blood red (#8B0000) and crimson (#DC143C)
- **Secondary**: Deep purple (#4B0082) and midnight blue (#191970)
- **Accent**: Gold (#FFD700) and silver (#C0C0C0)
- **Neutral**: Charcoal (#36454F) and bone white (#F5F5DC)

### Architectural Style
- **Victorian Gothic** with pointed arches and ornate details
- **Supernatural elements** integrated into traditional architecture
- **Atmospheric lighting** creating shadows and mystery
- **Decay and grandeur** side by side

## Key Locations

### Village Square
The starting location where players begin their journey. A small, close-knit community that serves as the gateway to larger adventures.

**Atmosphere**: Welcoming but mysterious
**Key NPCs**: Village Elder, Traveling Merchant
**Purpose**: Tutorial area and quest hub

### Crimson Palace
A massive Gothic structure that serves as the primary location for the first chapter. Its blood-red walls and imposing architecture create an atmosphere of both grandeur and foreboding.

**Atmosphere**: Eerie, blood-red, imposing
**Key NPCs**: Carmilla (the palace guardian)
**Purpose**: Main quest location and story progression

### Forest Paths
Connecting areas between major locations, these paths are filled with supernatural encounters and hidden secrets.

**Atmosphere**: Dark, mysterious, dangerous
**Key NPCs**: Various supernatural entities
**Purpose**: Exploration and side quests

## Supernatural Elements

### The Ravens
Mysterious entities that appear throughout the world, often serving as guides or harbingers of change. They are connected to the C story thread and represent the deeper mysteries of the world.

### Guardian Powers
Supernatural abilities that players can acquire through the A story thread. These powers are tied to the ancient guardians of the realm and provide both narrative and gameplay benefits.

### Faction Politics
The B story thread involves complex political relationships between different supernatural factions, each with their own goals and motivations.

## Character Archetypes

### The Village Elder
A wise figure who serves as the player's first guide and quest giver. Represents the traditional values of the community.

### Carmilla
The guardian of the Crimson Palace, a complex character who may be friend or foe depending on player choices. Represents the supernatural elements of the world.

### The Traveling Merchant
A mysterious figure who appears in various locations, offering goods and information. Represents the connection between different parts of the world.

## Narrative Themes

### Light vs. Darkness
The constant struggle between the mundane world and supernatural forces, represented through the visual contrast of light and shadow.

### Power and Responsibility
The acquisition of guardian powers comes with moral choices and consequences, exploring themes of power and its proper use.

### Community vs. Individual
The tension between serving the community and pursuing personal goals, reflected in the quest system and character relationships.

### Mystery and Discovery
The world is filled with secrets and hidden knowledge, encouraging exploration and investigation.

## Cultural Influences

### Gothic Literature
Drawing from classic Gothic novels like Dracula and Frankenstein, with their themes of supernatural horror and moral ambiguity.

### Victorian Society
The social structures and class systems of Victorian England, adapted to a fantasy setting.

### Folklore and Mythology
Elements from various European folklore traditions, particularly those involving ravens, guardians, and supernatural entities.

## World Rules

### Magic System
- **Guardian Powers**: Supernatural abilities acquired through quest completion
- **Faction Magic**: Different types of magic associated with different factions
- **Raven Mysteries**: Special abilities related to the C story thread

### Technology Level
- **Victorian Era**: Steam power, early electricity, mechanical devices
- **Supernatural Integration**: Magic and technology working together
- **Artisan Craftsmanship**: High-quality, hand-made items and architecture

### Social Structure
- **Class System**: Clear social hierarchies based on wealth and status
- **Supernatural Integration**: Supernatural entities integrated into society
- **Quest-Based Progression**: Advancement through completing quests and gaining recognition

## Atmosphere and Mood

### Eerie and Mysterious
The world should feel both beautiful and unsettling, with an underlying sense of unease and mystery.

### Gothic Grandeur
A sense of scale and importance, with architecture and landscapes that inspire awe and wonder.

### Blood-Red Themes
The color red appears throughout the world, representing both danger and power, life and death.

### Supernatural Presence
The world should feel alive with supernatural energy, even in the most mundane locations.

## Story Integration

### Quest Threads
Each of the three story threads (A/B/C) explores different aspects of the world:
- **A Story**: Guardian powers and ancient mysteries
- **B Story**: Faction politics and social dynamics
- **C Story**: Raven encounters and deeper mysteries

### Character Development
The world provides opportunities for character growth and moral development through the choices players make.

### Environmental Storytelling
The world itself tells stories through its architecture, lighting, and atmospheric details.

## Future Expansion

### Additional Chapters
The world is designed to support 18 total chapters, each exploring different aspects of the setting.

### New Locations
Each chapter can introduce new areas while maintaining the overall aesthetic and atmosphere.

### Deeper Mysteries
The world contains layers of mystery that can be gradually revealed through continued play.
